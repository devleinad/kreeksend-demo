How it works

Kreeksend is an email marketing platform. It enables you to create a valuable marketing list of prospects, partners, and clients, so you can develop relationships with them and build a responsive and profitable customer base. It has the deliverability rate of 99.9%, to make sure your messages reach destination inboxes

Add contacts

  • Create contact lists to manage your contact database
  • Import an existing contact list
  • Integrate your Kreeksend account with other services to set up recurring imports
  • Keep your contact lists growing and customer information up to date with landing pages, forms, and surveys
  • Use social media tools that give you an easy way to tap into the list-building power of social referrals

Plan marketing campaigns and manage sales

  • Use a drag-and-drop newsletter editor with ready-to use templates. You can also design your own template or use the HTML editor
  • Use autoresponders to follow up automatically with customers, providing info, offers, and all types of friendly communication.

  • Test out your marketing strategies with testing newsletters and landing pages.

Analyze results and plan new strategies

  • Search contacts and create unlimited segments based on specific demographic and customer behaviour criteria to manage your contact list and design marketing strategies that match the expectations of your customers.
  • Take advantage of analytics tools that enable you to create targeted offers to improve conversion rates
